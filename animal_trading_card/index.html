African Lion

A lion is known as "King of the Jungle".

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 20 - 40 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Habitat: Open plains, woodlands, thick bush and partial desert

African lions are considered close-knit and their social cirlce or "pride", referring to a group of lions, is made up of anywhere between 2 - 12 lions whom are closely related females and their cubs. Their prey are medium to large in size and includes animals like buffalos and zebras, and they most often hunt in groups.
